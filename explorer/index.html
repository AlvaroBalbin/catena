Catena

☍ ☍ ☍

Ask the Catholic tradition. Read the actual passages Aquinas wrote, verbatim and cited, and walk the chain of Scripture behind them - in English and the Latin Vulgate. It cites its sources, or it refuses. It never invents.

Try a question ("is God good"), a Scripture reference ("John 1:14", "Romans 5"), or a citation ("ST I, q.2, a.3").
Catena is an open, public-domain corpus: the Summa Theologica, the Douay-Rheims Bible, and the Clementine Vulgate, with a citation graph and a cite-or-refuse retrieval demo. This page runs entirely in your browser over that open data - nothing you type leaves it.  Source & data on GitHub ↗
